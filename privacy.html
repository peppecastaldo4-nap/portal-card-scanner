Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Welcome to Portal Card Scanner. Your privacy is fundamental to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our card identification services.

1. Data We Collect and Purposes

Card Images: When you scan a card, the image is sent to our systems. We use Artificial Intelligence exclusively to identify the card model and provide you with real-time market prices.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous data on the most used features to improve the accuracy of our recognition algorithm.

Device Identifiers: Used by advertising partners (Google AdMob) to display relevant ads.

2. Data Retention

We are proponents of "Data Minimization":

Images: We do not permanently store your images. Once identification is complete, the image is deleted from our temporary servers.

Statistical Data: Anonymized data on scan accuracy may be retained to train our computer vision models.

3. Third-Party Services and Affiliations

Advertising: We use Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use personal data (such as device advertising ID) to personalize ads.

Affiliate Links: The app contains links to TCGPlayer, Amazon, and eBay. If you purchase through these links, we may receive a commission. This does not incur additional costs for you, and we do not share your personal data with these vendors.

4. Security

We adopt standard encryption protocols to protect data during transfer between your device and our servers. However, remember that no internet transmission system is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: portalcardscanner@gmail.com
